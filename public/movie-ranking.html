Kristi's Favorite Movies

Willy Wonka and the Chocolate Factory

This is my family's favorite movie. We can quote the whole movie.

Barbie

It's new but it blew me away. I saw it four times in the theater and could watch over and over.

Breakfast at Tiffany's

It's a cliche but so charming and I wouldn't miss it while it was on.

Bonus: Wizard of Oz

It just reminds me of being young.
